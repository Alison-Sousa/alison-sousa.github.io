About Me

I am a Master's student in Computer Science at the University of São Paulo (USP), where I am advised by João Neto, PhD, Imperial College London. My research focuses on large-scale data science applied to sectoral and economic analysis, particularly in the context of policy evaluation and learning.

Before joining USP, I earned a degree from the Escola Superior de Propaganda e Marketing (ESPM-SP) (BA in International Relations), with international programs at Zuyd University of Applied Sciences and Meiji University. I also worked in Quantitative Analysis at China Construction Bank, the largest state-owned bank in Beijing. In addition, I am the Founder of SMTech, a technology startup specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science.
